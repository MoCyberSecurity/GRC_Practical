# Security Poster Examples

## Purpose
Provide visual cues for safe computing practices.

### Examples
1. **Password Hygiene**
   - “Use a passphrase, not a password! 3 words > 8 characters.”
2. **Phishing Awareness**
   - “Think Before You Click – Verify All Links.”
3. **Device Safety**
   - “Lock it, Don’t Lose it – Secure Your Devices.”

### Implementation
- Post digitally and physically across offices.
- Refresh posters quarterly to maintain attention.
- Link to training guides or intranet for more info.
