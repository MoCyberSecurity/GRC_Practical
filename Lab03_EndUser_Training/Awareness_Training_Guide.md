# End-User Awareness Training Guide

## Purpose
Reduce human risk by educating staff on cybersecurity best practices.

## Scope
All employees, contractors, and temporary staff.

## Training Topics

1. **Password Hygiene**
   - Use strong, unique passwords.
   - Enable MFA wherever possible.
   - Avoid password reuse across systems.

2. **Phishing & Social Engineering**
   - Recognize phishing emails, calls, and messages.
   - Verify links and attachments before clicking.
   - Report suspicious messages promptly.

3. **Data Handling & Privacy**
   - Follow data classification policies.
   - Encrypt sensitive files when sharing.
   - Dispose of confidential data securely.

4. **Device Security**
   - Keep devices updated and patched.
   - Lock screens when unattended.
   - Report lost or stolen devices.

5. **Incident Reporting**
   - Understand reporting procedures.
   - Know whom to contact and how.
   - Act promptly to minimize impact.

---

### Implementation Tips
- Conduct training quarterly or during onboarding.
- Use scenario-based exercises for engagement.
- Track completion rates and test knowledge retention.
