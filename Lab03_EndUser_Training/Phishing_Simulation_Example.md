# Phishing Simulation Example

## Purpose
Test staff awareness of phishing and social engineering.

## Scenario
- Mock email claiming “Important Payroll Update”
- Includes link to a fake login page (safe test environment)
- Metrics collected: Clicks, report rates, and time to report

## Observations & Recommendations
| Metric                 | Result | Action Plan                       |
|------------------------|--------|----------------------------------|
| Clicked link            | 20%    | Conduct follow-up training       |
| Reported email          | 60%    | Praise proactive reporting       |
| Failed to recognize     | 20%    | Include in next training session |

### Tips
- Never use real credentials in simulations.
- Redact sensitive data.
- Track results anonymously to respect privacy.
