# Risk Matrix Example

## Purpose
Visualise likelihood vs impact for prioritised risks.

### Risk Matrix (5x5 Scale)
| Impact\Likelihood | 1 Very Low | 2 Low | 3 Medium | 4 High | 5 Very High |
|------------------|------------|-------|----------|-------|-------------|
| 5 Very High      | Medium     | High  | High     | Critical | Critical   |
| 4 High           | Medium     | Medium| High     | High    | Critical   |
| 3 Medium         | Low        | Medium| Medium   | High    | High       |
| 2 Low            | Low        | Low   | Medium   | Medium  | High       |
| 1 Very Low       | Low        | Low   | Low      | Medium  | Medium     |

### Realistic Example
- Payroll DB Ransomware → Likelihood 5, Impact 5 → Critical
- CRM Phishing → Likelihood 3, Impact 4 → High
- Laptops Lost → Likelihood 3, Impact 3 → Medium
