# Sample Audit Findings

## Purpose
Demonstrate realistic audit outputs and recommendations for a small business environment.

## Scope
IT systems, data handling, and security controls.

---

### Executive Summary
- Audit revealed missing MFA for critical systems.
- Backup processes exist but not tested regularly.
- Endpoint security software is outdated on 20% of devices.

### Findings & Recommendations
| Finding ID | Description                                   | Risk Level | Recommendation                         | Owner  |
|------------|-----------------------------------------------|------------|----------------------------------------|-------|
| F01        | No MFA on CRM & Payroll systems               | High       | Enforce MFA on all critical systems    | IT     |
| F02        | Backup procedures not tested                  | Medium     | Perform quarterly DR tests             | IT     |
| F03        | Outdated antivirus on 5 laptops               | Medium     | Update and enforce patch management    | IT     |
| F04        | Employees unaware of phishing threats         | Medium     | Conduct monthly awareness training     | HR     |

### Notes
- All findings are redacted for privacy.
- Prioritize high-risk issues in remediation plan.
