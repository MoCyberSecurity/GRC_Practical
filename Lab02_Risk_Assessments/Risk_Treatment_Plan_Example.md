# Risk Treatment Plan Example

## Purpose
Demonstrate mitigation, transfer, acceptance, and avoidance strategies.

### Sample Treatment Plan
| Risk ID | Risk Description        | Risk Level | Treatment Strategy | Action Plan                                       | Owner  |
|---------|------------------------|------------|-----------------|-------------------------------------------------|-------|
| R01     | Payroll DB Ransomware  | Critical   | Mitigate        | Implement encrypted offline backups; test DR   | IT     |
| R02     | CRM Phishing           | High       | Mitigate        | MFA enabled; employee phishing simulation      | IT/HR  |
| R03     | Laptop Loss/Theft      | Medium     | Mitigate        | Full-disk encryption; secure storage guidance | IT     |
| R04     | Email Data Leak        | Medium     | Accept          | Monitor email usage; enforce DLP on sensitive files | IT     |
| R05     | Outdated software      | Medium     | Avoid           | Enforce automated patch management             | IT     |
