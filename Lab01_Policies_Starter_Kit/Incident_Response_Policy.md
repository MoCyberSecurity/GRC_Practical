# Incident Response Policy

## Purpose
Provide a structured approach to detect, respond, and recover from security incidents.

## Scope
Covers all IT systems, networks, and company data.

## Roles & Responsibilities
- **Incident Response Team (IRT):** Lead incident handling.
- **Employees:** Report incidents immediately via ticketing system or email.
- **Management:** Authorize actions, approve communication to stakeholders.

## Incident Handling Steps
1. **Preparation:** Tools, playbooks, training.
2. **Identification:** Detect incidents via SIEM, alerts, or user reports.
3. **Containment:** Limit spread; isolate affected systems.
4. **Eradication:** Remove malware or vulnerabilities.
5. **Recovery:** Restore services, validate integrity.
6. **Lessons Learned:** Update policies, train staff.

### Real-World Example
- Phishing email reported by employee triggers IRT. Suspicious attachments are quarantined, affected accounts reset, and users trained on phishing detection.

## Escalation & Communication
- Critical incidents reported to CTO and management within 30 minutes.
- Legal/PR teams notified for breaches affecting clients.
